Behind the Scenes

Our tool uses advanced technology to extract metadata while keeping your data private and secure.

Client-Side Processing

All metadata extraction happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

EXIF Technology

We use the EXIF.js library to read Exchangeable Image File Format data, which contains most of the metadata captured by digital cameras.

Fast Processing

Metadata extraction happens in milliseconds, providing you with instant results regardless of image size or complexity.

Cross-Platform

Works on all modern browsers and devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. No installation or registration required.
