Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data when using Exif Explorer.

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Exif Explorer is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly how your data is handled.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Exif Explorer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our image metadata extraction tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Important Note

Exif Explorer processes all images locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers. All metadata extraction happens entirely on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

A. Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: When you contact us through our contact form, we collect your name and email address.
  • Feedback: Any feedback, comments, or questions you send us.
  • Newsletter Subscription: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you interact with our website.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, operating system, and IP address (anonymized).
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for website functionality and analytics cookies.
What We Do NOT Collect
  • ✓ Your uploaded images (processed locally only)
  • ✓ Extracted metadata from your images
  • ✓ Personal identification beyond what you voluntarily provide
  • ✓ Sensitive personal information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our website
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our website
  • To understand and analyze how you use our website
  • To develop new products, services, features, and functionality
  • To communicate with you, including for customer service and updates
  • To send you emails (if you opt-in)
  • To find and prevent fraud

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure, or access.

Security Features of Exif Explorer
  • ✓ Client-side image processing (no server uploads)
  • ✓ HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • ✓ Regular security updates and monitoring
  • ✓ Secure coding practices and vulnerability testing

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Information, please contact us.
